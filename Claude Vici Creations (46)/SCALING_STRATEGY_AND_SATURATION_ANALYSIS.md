## ğŸ“‰ THE BREAKING POINT EXPLAINED

### What is Lead Saturation?

When you call the same population repeatedly:
1. **Calls 1-3:** Fresh reception, normal opt-in rate (~0.10-0.15%)
2. **Calls 4-6:** People recognize the number, opt-in rate drops (~0.08-0.10%)
3. **Calls 7-10:** Frustration sets in, opt-in rate drops (~0.05-0.08%)
4. **Calls 10+:** Anger/blocking, opt-in rate drops (<0.05%)

## ğŸ“Š MONITORING SATURATION IN FUTURE

**Run this query monthly to check campaign health:**

```sql
SELECT
    campaign_id,
    CASE
        WHEN called_count = 0 THEN 'Never called'
        WHEN called_count BETWEEN 1 AND 3 THEN 'Fresh'
        WHEN called_count BETWEEN 4 AND 6 THEN 'Moderate'
        WHEN called_count BETWEEN 7 AND 10 THEN 'Heavy'
        WHEN called_count > 10 THEN 'Exhausted'
    END as saturation_level,
    COUNT(*) as lead_count,
    ROUND(COUNT(*) * 100.0 / SUM(COUNT(*)) OVER (PARTITION BY campaign_id), 1) as percentage
FROM vicidial_list vl
JOIN vicidial_lists vls ON vl.list_id = vls.list_id
WHERE vls.campaign_id IN ('1022', '1027', '1028')
GROUP BY campaign_id, saturation_level
ORDER BY campaign_id, MIN(called_count);
```
